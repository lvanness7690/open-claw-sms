# Privacy Policy – OpenClaw SMS

Last updated: February 8, 2026

OpenClaw (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects limited personal information to provide SMS-based conversational assistance and account-related notifications.

## Information We Collect
We may collect:
- Mobile phone numbers
- Message content sent to and from OpenClaw
- Basic usage metadata (timestamps, delivery status)

## How We Use Information
Information is used solely to:
- Deliver requested SMS messages
- Respond to user-initiated conversations
- Provide task confirmations, reminders, and status updates

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. Message delivery is facilitated through trusted service providers strictly to operate the service.

## Data Retention
Message data is retained only as long as necessary to operate and improve the service or to comply with legal requirements.

## User Control
Users may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP**. For assistance, reply **HELP**.
