By opting in to OpenClaw SMS, you agree to receive conversational and account-related text messages from OpenClaw.

## Program Description
OpenClaw provides two-way SMS messaging for task assistance, confirmations, reminders, and status updates initiated by the user.

## Message Frequency
Message frequency varies based on user activity and requests.

## Fees
Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

## Opt-Out
You may opt out at any time by replying **STOP**.  
For help, reply **HELP** or contact support@openclaw.ai.

## Supported Carriers
OpenClaw SMS is supported on most major U.S. carriers. Delivery is not guaranteed.
